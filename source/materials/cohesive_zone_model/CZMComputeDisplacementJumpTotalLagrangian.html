CZMComputeDisplacementJumpTotalLagrangian

Compute the displacement jump increment across a czm interface in local coordinates for the Total Lagrangian kinematic formulation

Overview

The CZMComputeDisplacementJumpTotalLagrangian calculate the displacement jump across a cohesive zone in the interface natural coordinate system. This object assumes finite strains and does account for the interface rotation caused by deformations or rigid body motion.
