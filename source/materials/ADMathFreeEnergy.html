ADMathFreeEnergy

This material implements an analytical double well free energy

and its first order derivatives. Both provided properties furthermore come with forward mode automatic derivatives with respect to all degrees of freedom on the current element.

This free energy can be used with the AD version of the split Cahn-Hilliard equation (ADSplitCHWRes and ADSplitCHParsed).
