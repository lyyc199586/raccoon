SpecificHeatConductionTimeDerivative

Time derivative term of the heat equation with the specific heat and the density as arguments.

commentnote

This kernel uses the DerivativeMaterialInterface and accesses derivatives of the specific heat and density to build the Jacobian.

See also HeatCapacityConductionTimeDerivative.

  • variableThe name of the variable that this residual object operates on

    C++ Type:NonlinearVariableName

    Options:

    Description:The name of the variable that this residual object operates on
