CylindricalAverage

Compute a cylindrical average of a variableas a function of radius throughout the simulation domain.

Description

CylindricalAverage computes the average of multiple variables over cylindrical shells. The cylinder is defined by the midpoint and a vector along the cylinder axis. The cylindrical shells are defined by a maxiumum radius and the number of desired shells.

Input Parameters

  • cylinder_axisVector along cylinder coordinate axis

    C++ Type:libMesh::Point

    Options:

    Description:Vector along cylinder coordinate axis

  • radiusRadius to average out to

    C++ Type:double

    Options:

    Description:Radius to average out to
