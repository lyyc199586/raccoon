Calculate location of grain boundaries in a polycrystalline sample

This AuxKernel computes the value as

where is the variable the AuxKernel is acting on and (v) are a set of couple order parameters. When used with order parameters of the polycrystalline grain growth model, the resulting field for is in grain interiors and in grain boundaries ( along the mid plan of the grain boundaries).
